KodaPure

KodaPure is a magnetic-bead DNA extraction kit for routine research sample preparation.

KodaPure supports manual DNA extraction from tissue, blood, buffy coat, and cultured cells, with a manual magnetic-bead workflow and direct access to the quick protocol and SDS.

Representative specifications

Specifications and workflow.

  • Up to 25 mg tissue or 200 µL whole blood
  • About 45 to 60 minutes for 12 manual preparations
  • A260/A280 typically 1.8 to 2.0
  • Compatible with PCR, qPCR, ddPCR, NGS, and Sanger workflows
Supported samples

Supported samples for routine manual extraction.

KodaPure supports the sample types below in the same manual magnetic-bead workflow.

Sample type Input limit Notes
Fresh or frozen tissue Up to 25 mg Suitable for manual extraction from common tissue inputs.
Whole blood Up to 200 µL Uses the same manual bench workflow as other supported samples.
Buffy coat Supported Runs in the same magnetic-bead workflow format.
Cultured cells Up to 5 × 106 cells Supports routine cultured-cell DNA extraction work.

Manual magnetic-bead workflow.

Tube-based bench workflow for routine DNA extraction work.

Workflow

Routine manual bench format.

  • Tube-based preparation with magnetic separation
  • Adjustable elution from 50 to 200 µL
  • Same manual workflow across tissue, blood, buffy coat, and cultured-cell inputs
  • Built for day-to-day bench handling and routine quality checks
Pack sizes

Available pack sizes.
