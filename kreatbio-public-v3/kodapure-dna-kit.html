Method Architecture

A bead-based workflow framed as a product system for serious research teams.

01

Lysis and sample conditioning

Sample preparation stages are framed around consistent nucleic acid release and controlled chemical conditions before bead interaction.

02

Magnetic-bead capture and wash

DNA binds to bead surfaces, enabling wash cycles that remove unwanted material while keeping the workflow structured and operator-friendly.

03

Elution and downstream handoff

Purified DNA is recovered in a form positioned for common research workflows such as amplification, sequencing preparation, and teaching labs.
