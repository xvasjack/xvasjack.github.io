Workflow and specs

Main review points for KodaPure.

The public page gives enough detail for a first review without forcing people through filler copy.

Workflow

Manual magnetic-bead workflow.

  • Tube-based preparation with magnetic separation
  • About 45 to 60 minutes for 12 manual preparations
  • Adjustable elution from 50 to 200 µL
  • Built for day-to-day bench handling and QC review
Representative specs

Public values used for first review.

  • A260/A280 typically 1.8 to 2.0
  • A260/A230 typically above 1.7
  • Typical downstream use includes PCR, qPCR, ddPCR, NGS, and Sanger work
  • Storage details are confirmed in the technical sheet during quote review
