KodaPure

KodaPure is a magnetic-bead DNA extraction kit for routine research sample preparation.

KodaPure supports DNA extraction from bacteria, animal tissue, and soil or dirty samples for a streamlined, column-free process.

Workflow

How magnetic-bead extraction works.

Magnetic-bead steps replace column handling and reduce centrifuge-heavy bench work.

01

Lyse the sample

Break open bacteria, tissue, or soil-linked material to release nucleic acids into solution.

02

Bind DNA to magnetic beads

DNA attaches to the beads instead of moving through a spin column.

03

Wash while the magnet holds the beads

Use the magnet to keep the DNA-bound beads in place while unwanted material is removed.

04

Elute the DNA

Release purified DNA for downstream work such as PCR, qPCR, and sequencing.
