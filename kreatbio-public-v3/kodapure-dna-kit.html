Workflow

Magnetic-bead capture from lysis through clean elution.

Lysis

Break open the sample and release nucleic acid into solution.

Bead binding

Mix magnetic beads with the lysate so DNA attaches to the particle surface.

Wash

Use a magnet to hold DNA in place while removing supernatant and wash carryover away.

Elute

Release the purified DNA into fresh buffer for downstream molecular work.

Kit components
