FAQ

Common buyer questions

These are the questions that usually come up before a trial, a purchase review, or a training discussion.

What sample types does KodaPure support?

Representative inputs include fresh or frozen tissue, whole blood, buffy coat, and cultured cells. Final limits are confirmed in the technical sheet.

What is the minimum and maximum sample input?

Typical screening ranges are up to 25 mg tissue, 200 uL whole blood, and up to 5 x 10^6 cultured cells.

Can KodaPure DNA be used for Nanopore sequencing?

That application should be reviewed case by case during technical evaluation because sample quality and fragment goals matter.

Can KodaGeno work with institutional LMS platforms such as Canvas or Moodle?

Institution deployment discussions can cover learner routing, instructor access, and how the program is handed off inside an academic environment.

Can KodaGeno lessons be accessed without an institution account?

Yes. Individual learners can start from the live lesson library on kodageno.com.

How long does a typical KodaGeno module take?

Modules are designed as lesson-sized units that fit guided classroom delivery or self-directed practice.

How are SDS and CoA requests handled?

Use the contact form, choose the request type, and expect a response from the commercial team within 2 business days.
