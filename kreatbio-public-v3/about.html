Founding story

Why KreatBio exists.

KreatBio was shaped around a common bottleneck in research and academic environments: wet-lab teams can generate samples, but many groups still struggle to train new analysts fast enough to handle sequencing outputs well. The company was built to connect those two workflow points instead of treating them as separate procurement problems.

That is why the product strategy pairs a wet-lab preparation system with a guided learning platform rather than building a catalogue of unrelated tools.

What the company builds

Two products with one workflow logic.

  • KodaPure for magnetic-bead DNA extraction in research sample preparation
  • KodaGeno for guided sequencing analysis training in academic and institutional settings
  • Partnership programs for cohort delivery, branded rollout, and program review
