About

KreatBio was built to close the gap between wet-lab preparation and sequencing analysis training.

Many teams can prepare DNA. Fewer teams can teach new analysts what to do next with confidence. KreatBio brings those two problems into one company by pairing KodaPure sample preparation with KodaGeno guided analysis training.

Mission

Make sample preparation and sequencing analysis training easier to adopt, easier to teach, and easier to review.

KreatBio focuses on practical wet-lab preparation, clearer learner onboarding, and documents that support both scientific review and procurement review.

Founding story

Why KreatBio exists.

KreatBio was shaped around a common bottleneck in research and academic environments: wet-lab teams can generate samples, but many groups still struggle to train new analysts fast enough to handle sequencing outputs well. The company was built to connect those two workflow points instead of treating them as separate procurement problems.

That is why KreatBio pairs a wet-lab preparation system with a guided learning platform instead of building a catalogue of unrelated tools.

Current offer

What KreatBio offers today.

KreatBio currently offers one research-use extraction kit, one sequencing-analysis training platform, and the review paths buyers use to assess both.

Product line KodaPure DNA extraction and KodaGeno sequencing-analysis training are the two active public offerings today.

KodaPure covers sample preparation. KodaGeno covers guided sequencing-analysis training for learners and institutions.

Public documents Technical pack, company brief, quick protocol, module overview, and public SDS are available now.

Those files give scientific and procurement reviewers something concrete to open before they contact KreatBio.

Commercial path Quote requests, technical sheet requests, CoA by lot number, and partnership discussions are handled through the contact form.

That gives labs and academic programs one place to start a document, pricing, or rollout discussion.

Current stage KreatBio welcomes pilot evaluations and institutional rollout discussions for its research-use products and training.
