About

KreatBio is the company behind KodaPure and KodaGeno.

KreatBio develops KodaPure for research DNA extraction and KodaGeno for guided sequencing analysis training.

See KodaPure See KodaGeno
Company facts

Based in Kuala Lumpur.

  • KodaPure: Manual DNA extraction for routine research sample preparation.
  • KodaGeno: Guided training for sequencing analysis.
  • Research use only: KreatBio products are not intended for diagnostic procedures.
Current products

What KreatBio offers.

KodaPure supports wet-lab sample preparation. KodaGeno supports sequencing analysis training.

KodaPure

DNA extraction for routine research work.

KodaPure is a manual magnetic-bead kit for tissue, blood, buffy coat, and cultured cells.

KodaGeno

Guided training for sequencing analysis.

KodaGeno supports bacterial WGS, amplicon, and RNA-seq learning for courses, programs, and self-study.
