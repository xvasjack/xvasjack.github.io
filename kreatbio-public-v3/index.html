Product overview

Two products, two jobs, one supplier relationship.

KodaPure handles sample preparation. KodaGeno trains the people who need to analyze what comes out of it.

KodaPure

Magnetic-bead DNA extraction for routine research sample preparation.

KodaPure is designed for tissue, blood, buffy coat, and cultured cell inputs, with adjustable elution and downstream compatibility for amplification and sequencing workflows.

Up to 25 mg tissue / 200 uL blood A260/A280 typically 1.8-2.0 PCR / qPCR / NGS / Sanger

KodaGeno

Interactive sequencing analysis training for bacterial WGS, amplicon, and RNA-seq.

KodaGeno gives learners a scenario, asks them to choose the next analysis step, and explains how to read the result. Institutions can run it as a cohort program, and individuals can start from the lesson library.

Who it is for

Built for teams preparing samples, teaching analysis, or scaling training programs.

Each buyer group comes in with a different problem. The page should answer that problem quickly.

Wet-lab teams

Need a DNA extraction workflow that is easy to evaluate and easy to repeat.

KodaPure gives wet-lab teams a magnetic-bead method, practical document set, and clear downstream fit for PCR and sequencing work.

Academic programs

Need a ready-made way to teach sequencing analysis without building lessons from scratch.

KodaGeno provides guided scenarios, lesson flow, and instructor-facing delivery for departments and cohort-based programs.

Training institutions

Need branded delivery, cohort oversight, and a program that scales past one workshop.

Partnership programs cover onboarding, learner routing, instructor access, reporting, and annual review planning.
