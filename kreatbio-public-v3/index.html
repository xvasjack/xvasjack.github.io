About us

KreatBio develops practical research tools for DNA extraction and sequencing analysis training.

We build products for research labs, universities, and training programs that need usable workflows and clearer learning tools.

KodaPure supports routine manual DNA extraction across common sample types. KodaGeno gives guided sequencing analysis practice, with bacterial WGS live now and more lesson areas in development.

Products

Choose between KodaPure and KodaGeno.

KodaPure

DNA extraction kit for routine research work.

KodaPure is a manual magnetic-bead DNA extraction kit for tissue, blood, buffy coat, and cultured cells.

  • Tissue, blood, buffy coat, and cultured cells
  • Manual workflow with representative timing and specifications
  • Quick protocol and SDS available
See KodaPure
KodaGeno

Sequencing analysis training for classes, teams, and learners.

KodaGeno is a guided sequencing analysis training product for courses, programs, and individual learners.

  • Bacterial WGS lessons are live now, with amplicon and RNA-seq coming soon
  • Built for universities, training programs, and self-study
  • Guided lessons, reporting, and institutional partnership options
