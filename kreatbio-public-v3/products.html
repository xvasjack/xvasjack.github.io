At a glance

Two products, two jobs.

  • KodaPure is for tissue, blood, buffy coat, and cultured cell DNA extraction.
  • KodaGeno is for guided sequencing-analysis training in classes, teams, or self-study.

Flagship product

KodaPure DNA Extraction Kit

Magnetic-bead DNA extraction for tissue, blood, buffy coat, and cultured cell workflows in research settings.

Up to 25 mg tissue / 200 µL blood 45-60 min for 12 manual preps PCR / qPCR / ddPCR / NGS / Sanger
  • Representative yield and purity ranges included
  • Quick protocol, public SDS download, and separate request paths for technical sheet and CoA
  • Tube-based manual workflow for wet-lab evaluation and procurement review

Training platform

KodaGeno

Interactive sequencing analysis training for bacterial WGS, amplicon, and RNA-seq analysis with institution and direct learner access.

Interactive terminal Guided decisions WGS / amplicon / RNA-seq
  • Guided practice for students, postdocs, and wet-lab scientists moving into analysis work
  • Department and program access for semester courses, bootcamps, and training teams
  • Direct learner access through the live lesson library
