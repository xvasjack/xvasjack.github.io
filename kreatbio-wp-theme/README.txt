KreatBio Authority Theme (WordPress)

What this does
- Applies the full biotech redesign directly in WordPress.
- Creates and publishes these pages on activation: Home, Products, KreatPure DNA Kit, KodaGeno Learning Platform, Resources, About, Contact.
- Sets Home as the front page automatically.

Install on staging
1) WordPress Admin -> Appearance -> Themes -> Add New -> Upload Theme.
2) Upload kreatbio-authority-theme.zip.
3) Click Activate.
4) Open stage.kreatbio.com and hard refresh.

Notes
- Contact form is visual only until connected to your actual form plugin/backend.
- Keep only claims backed by your current documents and evidence.
